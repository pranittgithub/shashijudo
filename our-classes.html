OUR JUDO SUCCESS STORY

Journey of Discipline, Growth, and Victory

Early Training

Started Judo at Friends Judo Club under Arjun Awardee Billimorya Sensei. Over 22 years of passionate training and dedication building the foundation of excellence.

"The journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step. Every champion starts with dedication and discipline."

Teaching Experience

Taught Judo in BMC schools, blind schools, NGOs like Haseema Trust, Apun Ka Club, Bal Jeevan Trust, and many more. Spreading the art of discipline and self-defense.

"Teaching is not just about technique, it's about inspiring others to believe in themselves and reach their potential."
